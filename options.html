Regex Replace Help


Arguments are separated by newlines, and search/replace terms are separated by three pipe symbols.
For example: 'apple ||| orange' would replace "I like to eat apples." with "I like to eat oranges."
More complex Regex substitutions are also possible: 'quick brown(.*)lazy dog ||| lazy brown\1quick dog' would replace 'The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog.' with "The lazy fox jumped over the quick brown dog." and '[0-9]+ ||| 0' would replace any digit (or string of continuing digits) with 0. The source for this extension can be found on my GitHub.
